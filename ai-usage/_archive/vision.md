# Findemy – Complete Vision & Product Overview

## Introduction

**Findemy** is a discovery and community platform designed for skill-based learning.

It helps users easily find academies related to their interests while also helping academies gain visibility, connect with students, and grow digitally.

Today, when someone wants to learn a skill like:

* Guitar
* Singing
* Dance
* Kathak
* Photography
* Art
* Yoga
* Martial Arts

they usually search on Google using terms like:

* “Guitar academy near me”
* “Best singing classes nearby”
* “Dance classes in Delhi”

The current experience is frustrating and inefficient because users need to:

* Browse through endless Google results
* Open multiple websites
* Call academies manually
* Ask about fees and timings
* Compare batches individually
* Check reviews across platforms
* Verify whether seats are available

This process is time-consuming and unorganized.

Findemy aims to solve this problem by creating one centralized platform where users can discover, compare, and connect with academies easily.

# How Findemy Works

## 1. Academy Discovery

Users can search for academies based on:

* Skill or interest
* Location
* Fees
* Batch timings
* Ratings and reviews
* Experience level
* Available workshops

For example:

* Guitar classes nearby
* Kathak academy in Delhi
* Beginner singing classes
* Weekend dance workshops

Instead of manually searching across Google, users get everything inside one platform.

# Academy Profiles

Every academy gets a dedicated profile containing:

* Academy information
* Courses offered
* Batch timings
* Fees
* Ratings and reviews
* Photos/videos
* Contact information
* Demo class availability
* Workshop listings

This creates transparency and improves decision-making for students.
