# 3. The Solution

## 3.1 In one line

**Findemy puts academy discovery, transparent academy info, trial booking, and enrolment in one place — and gives academies the tools to be found and to run their classes.**

It collapses the messy multi-tab, multi-call process into a single guided flow, and replaces "call to find out" with transparent, standardised information.

## 3.2 Value to students

- **One place to discover** academies by skill, location, fees, timings, ratings, and level.
- **Transparency** — fees, batch timings, modes (online/offline), reviews, and mentor info are visible up front on every academy profile, so learners can evaluate options without calling around.
- **Low-risk trials** — book a **trial class** in a few taps instead of cold-calling.
- **A clear path to commit** — after a good trial, enrol in a regular batch without leaving the app.
- **Workshops & events** — discover and register for one-off masterclasses, demos, and events.

## 3.3 Value to academies

- **Get discovered** by learners actively looking nearby — not random foot traffic.
- **A real profile** — courses, batches, fees, photos, reviews, and demo availability in a credible, consistent format.
- **Fill batches** — convert trial-takers into enrolled students.
- **Run the basics** — manage trials, attendance, batches, and enrolments from the academy (admin) app.
- **Workshops as a growth lever** — host masterclasses/demos to attract and convert new learners.

## 3.4 How it works

Findemy is built around a simple five-step arc. The MVP delivers the first four; the fifth is the long-term direction.

1. **Discover** — Search and browse academies by skill, location, ratings, and more.
2. **Evaluate** — Weigh academies using transparent, standardised info (fees, timings, experience, reviews) and pick the best fit.
3. **Connect** — Book a trial class (or register for a workshop). See and contact the academy.
4. **Learn & Grow** — Attend the trial, leave a review, and enrol in a regular batch. Keep learning via batches and workshops.
5. **Be Part of a Community** *(future)* — Showcase progress, get recognised, and connect with other learners and creators.

## 3.5 The core wedge: trial-first

The heart of the product is the **discover → try → enrol** loop. The trial is the hinge: it's low-commitment for the learner and a qualified, paying lead for the academy. Everything in the MVP exists to make that loop smooth and trustworthy. Community, content, and competitions are deliberately deferred so the core loop can be proven first (see [`05-mvp-scope.md`](05-mvp-scope.md)).
