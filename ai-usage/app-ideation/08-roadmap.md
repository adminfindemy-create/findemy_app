# 8. Roadmap

A phased view of where Findemy goes. Phases are about **sequence and focus**, not fixed dates. Each phase should be substantially proven before leaning into the next.

## Phase 0 — MVP (now)
**Goal:** prove the core loop in one city.

- Market: **Delhi-NCR** only.
- Core loop: **discover → try → enrol**.
- Modules: discovery & search, academy profiles, trial booking + payments, enrolment, workshops, events, reviews, profile; academy (admin) app for intake & management.
- Revenue: **commission** on trials/enrolments.
- Success looks like: real academies with real trial supply, learners completing the loop, healthy trial→enrol conversion.

## Phase 1 — Deepen & monetise
**Goal:** strengthen the marketplace and open new revenue.

- **More revenue streams:** academy listing fees, premium/featured listings, **student passes** (discount memberships).
- Better discovery (ranking, personalisation), richer academy tooling, retention features.
- **Geographic expansion:** extend beyond Delhi-NCR to other metros once the playbook is repeatable.

## Phase 2 — Community & creator ecosystem
**Goal:** make skills social; build engagement and a reason to return beyond transactions.

- **Reels & creator ecosystem** — learners upload short-form content of their journey; academies gain organic exposure.
- **Leaderboards** — academy / city / national rankings to drive participation and consistency.
- **Findemy-led events & competitions** — talent hunts, dance battles, open mics, skill festivals, city- and national-level championships, run by Findemy.
- **Recognition & rewards** — "Top performer of the month," featured creators, awards, judging/guest opportunities.

## Long-term vision

> To become India's largest community-driven platform for discovering academies, learning skills, showcasing talent, and building creator-driven learning communities.

Findemy aims to transform skill learning from a fragmented offline process into an engaging, social, and rewarding digital ecosystem — a place where **students discover opportunities, academies grow, creators build audiences, talent gets recognised, and communities form around shared passions.**

---

*Phases 2 and the community features are the ambition that makes Findemy more than a directory — but they are deliberately sequenced after the core marketplace is working. See [`05-mvp-scope.md`](05-mvp-scope.md) for what that means for today.*
