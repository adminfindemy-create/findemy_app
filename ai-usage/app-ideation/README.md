# Findemy — Product Documentation

> **Discover your Art.**
> Findemy is India's discovery platform for skill academies. It helps people find, choose, and try local academies for music, dance, art, yoga, fitness and more — and helps academies get discovered, fill their batches, and grow online.

This folder is the **canonical, non-technical source of truth** for what Findemy is, who it's for, the problem it solves, what's in the MVP, and how the business works. It is written for the internal team (founders, engineers, designers) to stay aligned.

**Status:** MVP in active build · Launch market: **Delhi-NCR** · Stage: pre-launch (as of 2026-06-17).
