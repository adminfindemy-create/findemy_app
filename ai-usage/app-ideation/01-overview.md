# 1. Overview

## What Findemy is

**Findemy is a discovery and booking platform for skill academies.** When someone wants to learn a skill — guitar, singing, dance, kathak, photography, art, yoga, martial arts — Findemy is the one place to find local academies, see clear and transparent information about each, book a trial class, and enrol. For academies, it's the place to get discovered by nearby learners, fill their batches, and run their classes.

**Tagline:** *Discover your Art.*

## The one-liner

> Findemy is "the easiest way to find, try, and join a skill academy near you" — and for academies, "the simplest way to get discovered and fill your classes."

## Elevator pitch

Learning a skill offline is huge in India, but *finding the right place to learn* is still stuck in the pre-internet era: people Google around, open a dozen tabs, call academies one by one, and ask the same questions about fees, timings, and trials. Academies, meanwhile, depend on word-of-mouth and have little online presence.

Findemy turns that fragmented hunt into a clean, app-based experience. Learners discover academies by skill and location, see transparent info on fees, timings, and reviews, book a **trial class** in a few taps, and — if they like it — **enrol**. Academies manage their profile, batches, trials, attendance, and enrolments from a companion app. Findemy earns a commission when a learner books a trial or enrols.

The long-term ambition is bigger than discovery: a community where learners showcase their progress, talent gets recognised, and skills become social. But the MVP stays tightly focused on the core loop — **discover → try → enrol** — in one city.

## Who it's for (at a glance)

- **Learners** of all kinds: adults learning for passion, parents booking classes for their kids, serious/aspiring performers, and self-driven teens. (See [`04-users.md`](04-users.md).)
- **Academies**: from independent gurus and small local studios to established institutes that want digital reach.

## Current stage

- **MVP in active build.** Two apps: a **student app** (learners) and an **admin/academy app** (academies), backed by a shared API.
- **Launch market: Delhi-NCR.** One city first, to build dense academy supply and learner demand together before expanding to other metros.
- **Categories:** music, dance, art, yoga, fitness, and other creative/performing skills.
- Community features (reels, leaderboards, Findemy-run competitions) are **explicitly out of the MVP** — they come later. See [`08-roadmap.md`](08-roadmap.md).

## Brand statement

> Findemy is not just an app. It's a movement to discover, learn, showcase, and grow together.
