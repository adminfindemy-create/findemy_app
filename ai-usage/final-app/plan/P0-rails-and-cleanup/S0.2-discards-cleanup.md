## 2. ⚠️ Critical caveat — do NOT remove the `Slot` model in P0
`Booking` and `Trial` hold FK relations to `Slot`, and `bookings`/`trials` services read it. Removing the `Slot` Prisma model + `slots` module now leaves `backend/api` non-compiling until S2.1 reworks trial availability to `capacity − enrolled`.
- **S0.2 removes only isolated dead scope** (below). **Leave `Slot`/`SlotStatus`/`slots` module in place.**
- The `Slot` teardown is **scheduled as step 1 of S2.1** (so it's removed *and* replaced in one change, no long broken-build window). Track it there.
