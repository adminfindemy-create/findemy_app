## 3. The checklist (per screen)
- **Kit:** persimmon primary (jade only as a secondary accent), Instrument Serif display + Geist body, warm paper, rounded cards — matches the prototype.
- **A11y:** visible focus rings; `prefers-reduced-motion` honoured on transitions; **≥44px** touch targets; `accessibilityLabel`/`accessibilityRole` on icon-only controls (star rating, QR scan, join/leave, tab icons); colour is never the *only* signal (tier badges carry text, not just colour); tabular figures on money/%.
- **Copy:** Delhi-NCR content; no placeholder lorem; error states have human copy.

## 4. Component design, per side
- **Both apps:** no new components — apply the shared `@findemy/ui` tokens/atoms already used elsewhere. Where a net-new screen hand-rolled a style (e.g., the tier badge, the live pill, the QR box), confirm it reads from `theme.color`/`theme.font` (most already do) and add the missing `accessibilityLabel`s. The icon-only controls (star rating in `review.tsx`, the Classes tab grad-cap icon, scan/join buttons) are the most likely to lack labels — add them.
